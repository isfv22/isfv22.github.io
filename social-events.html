Social events

ISFV22 will combine scientific sessions with social and networking activities in Madrid.

Welcome reception

The welcome reception is planned for the first day, together with opening activities and the Masters of Flow Visualization event.

Conference banquet

Madrid offers outstanding venues for the conference banquet, including historical and cultural spaces in the city center. Final venue TBC.

Networking activities

Networking activities may include a cultural visit or group event designed to support scientific exchange and collaborations.

Madrid setting

Social and networking programme

ISFV22 will take advantage of Madrid’s central cultural landscape to create informal opportunities for discussion, networking and scientific exchange beyond the technical sessions.

The conference banquet and committee dinner will be organized in centrally located venues selected for their capacity, accessibility, atmosphere and connection with the city’s cultural life. Candidate options considered during the proposal phase include landmark cultural spaces and restaurants combining Spanish gastronomy with cultural experiences.

Final venues and practical details will be announced before registration opens.
