Social events

Welcome reception

The welcome reception will be held on Monday evening, opening the social programme of the symposium.

Conference banquet

The conference banquet will take place on Wednesday in a centrally located Madrid venue. Final venue to be confirmed.

Social activities

Social activities run in parallel on Tuesday (alongside the committee dinner) and on Thursday (alongside the short courses), and may include cultural visits and group events that support scientific exchange.

Madrid setting

Social and networking programme

ISFV22 will take advantage of Madrid’s central cultural landscape to create informal opportunities for discussion, networking and scientific exchange beyond the technical sessions.

The welcome reception (Monday evening), the ISFV Committee dinner (Tuesday), the conference banquet (Wednesday) and the parallel social activities (Tuesday and Thursday) will take place in centrally located venues selected for their capacity, accessibility, atmosphere and connection with the city’s cultural life.

Final venues and practical details will be announced before registration opens.
