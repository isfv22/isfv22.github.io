Abstract submission

Call for abstracts

Submit your contribution

Participants will be invited to submit abstracts related to the ISFV22 scientific scope, including applications, methods and techniques, and data analysis and visualization.

Abstracts will undergo review by the scientific committee. Detailed instructions, templates and the online submission platform will be made available in due course.

Selected contributions may be invited for submission to special issues in international journals, subject to confirmation.

The best abstracts will be selected for extended 30-minute contributions.

Submission platform: not yet available.
Contact: isfv22@uc3m.es

Review process

Submissions will be evaluated for scientific quality, relevance to flow visualization and clarity of presentation.
