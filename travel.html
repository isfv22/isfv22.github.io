Travel and accommodation

By air

Madrid-Barajas Adolfo Suárez International Airport is one of Europe’s major hubs, with direct connections to many international destinations. It is linked to the city center by metro, the Cercanías train, airport express bus and taxi. For more on reaching the city, see the official guide to getting to Madrid.

By metro and bus

The Campus Madrid - Puerta de Toledo is well connected by Madrid public transport. The nearest metro station is Puerta de Toledo (Line 5), and several bus lines stop nearby. Detailed routes will be added before the conference. For full public-transport information, see getting around Madrid.

Accommodation

Recommended hotels and suggested areas to stay in Madrid will be published closer to registration opening. The campus is central and well served by public transport, so a wide range of neighbourhoods is convenient. For general information on where to stay, see the official Madrid accommodation guide.
