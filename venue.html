Venue and Madrid

ISFV22 will be hosted at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, Puerta de Toledo Campus, in the city center.

Conference venue

UC3M Puerta de Toledo Campus

The Puerta de Toledo Campus is located in central Madrid and offers modern conference facilities suitable for scientific exchange, including an auditorium, several meeting rooms and spaces for parallel sessions and networking.

The campus is close to cultural heritage sites and parks, and is well connected by metro, bus and train.

About Madrid

Welcome to Madrid

Madrid is the capital of Spain and of the Madrid region, with approximately 3.4 million inhabitants. The city combines strong academic activity, major public and private research centers, world-renowned museums, green areas and excellent transport connections.

Highlights include Parque del Buen Retiro, Casa de Campo, Museo Reina Sofía, Museo del Prado and Museo Thyssen-Bornemisza. The Madrid region includes several UNESCO World Heritage sites, with additional destinations easily reachable by public transport.

By air

Madrid-Barajas International Airport is one of Europe’s major hubs and offers direct connections to many international destinations. Taxi and public transport connections to the city center are available.

By metro and bus

The campus is well connected by Madrid public transport. Specific routes and station information will be added before the conference.
