Campus Madrid - Puerta de Toledo

The Campus Madrid - Puerta de Toledo is located in central Madrid and offers modern conference facilities suitable for scientific exchange, including an auditorium, several meeting rooms and spaces for parallel sessions and networking.

The campus is close to cultural heritage sites and parks, and is well connected by metro, bus and train.
